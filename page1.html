Maruya Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4 saba bananas, sliced lengthwise
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • Oil for frying

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the batter by mixing flour, water, and sugar.
  2. Coat the banana slices in the batter.
  3. Fry until golden brown. Serve warm.
Champorado Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup glutinous rice
  • 4 cups water
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • Condensed milk (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Boil the rice in water until soft.
  2. Add cocoa powder and sugar, stirring continuously.
  3. Top with condensed milk before serving, if desired.
Sinigang Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g pork or shrimp
  • 1 packet tamarind mix
  • 1 cup radish slices
  • 1 cup kangkong leaves
  • 1 liter water

Instructions:

  1. Boil pork or shrimp in water until tender.
  2. Add tamarind mix and vegetables, cook until soft.
  3. Serve hot with rice.
Adobo Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken or pork
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
  • 5 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp peppercorns

Instructions:

  1. Marinate meat in soy sauce, vinegar, and garlic.
  2. Cook on medium heat until tender and sauce thickens.
  3. Serve with steamed rice.
Kare-Kare Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 500g oxtail
  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup bagoong
  • 1 cup banana blossoms
  • 1 liter water

Instructions:

  1. Boil oxtail until tender.
  2. Add peanut butter and banana blossoms, cook until thick.
  3. Serve with bagoong and rice.
Lechon Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole pig
  • 2 cups soy sauce
  • 1 cup calamansi juice
  • 5 garlic cloves, minced
  • Banana leaves

Instructions:

  1. Marinate pig in soy sauce, calamansi, and garlic.
  2. Stuff with herbs, wrap in banana leaves.
  3. Roast until skin is crispy.
